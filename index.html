About Me

Who I Am

Hi, I'm Angel Cerecedo, a dedicated business consultant with a passion for helping companies grow through tailored digital solutions. With expertise in web design, app development, marketing, analytics, and branding, I work closely with clients to understand their unique needs and craft strategies that drive real results. Whether you're a startup looking to establish a strong online presence or a well-established business aiming to optimize performance, I offer a personalized approach to help you achieve your goals. I believe in building lasting partnerships with my clients, offering hands-on support and creative solutions that make an impact. Let’s work together to bring your business vision to life.

My Services

Web design

I specialize in creating user-friendly, responsive, and visually appealing websites tailored to your business needs. From concept to launch, I ensure your website aligns with your brand identity and delivers a seamless user experience to drive engagement and conversions.

App Development

I develop custom mobile and web applications designed to streamline your operations and enhance customer experiences. My focus is on creating scalable, secure, and intuitive apps that meet your business goals and keep your users coming back.

Analystics

help businesses harness the power of data with advanced analytics. By collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data, I provide actionable insights to improve decision-making, optimize strategies, and drive growth for your business.

Marketing

My marketing services are designed to boost your online presence and connect with your target audience effectively. From SEO and social media management to email campaigns and paid advertising, I create personalized strategies to grow your brand and increase revenue.

eCommerce

I build robust eCommerce platforms that provide a seamless shopping experience for your customers. Whether you're launching a new online store or optimizing an existing one, my solutions are designed to increase conversions and maximize sales.

Content Development

Content is king. I develop high-quality, engaging content that resonates with your audience and strengthens your brand voice. Whether it’s blog articles, product descriptions, or social media posts, I create content that captivates and converts.

Branding

I craft strong, memorable brand identities that set you apart from the competition. From logo design and brand messaging to visual guidelines, I help create a cohesive brand that reflects your values and connects with your customers.

Video & Photography

Visual storytelling is a powerful tool in today’s digital landscape. My professional video and photography services capture your brand’s essence, create lasting impressions, and help you stand out in a crowded market. I produce content that not only looks great but also aligns with your business goals.
